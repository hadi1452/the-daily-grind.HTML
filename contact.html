Before You Write

Common Questions

Do you take reservations?

We're walk-in only during regular hours. For private events and group bookings (10+), use the form above and select "Event Booking."

Do you have dairy-free and vegan options?

Yes — oat, almond, and soy milk are available for all drinks at no extra charge. Check our menu for full dietary labels.

Can I bring my laptop and work here?

Absolutely. We have free high-speed WiFi and plenty of outlets. We just ask that you order something every couple of hours — keeps the vibes right.

Do you sell your coffee beans?

We sell our house blends in-store and ship within the state. Wholesale inquiries welcome — use the form and select "Wholesale / Beans."

Is the space dog-friendly?

Dogs are welcome on our outdoor patio! Leashed, well-mannered pups get a free puppuccino. Inside is human-only per health code.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes — physical gift cards are available at the counter in any amount. Digital gift cards are coming soon.
